About

Social Bible Wiki could be considered in two different parts: social networking service and the wiki-based bible.

Wiki-based bible

Unlike the traditional wiki-based websites, which let the user create pages freely. We instead fill the site with bible and each page represents one verse in the bible. The bible in different languages will be imported in order to provide multi-language interfaces. Currently we decided to support English and Traditional Chinese.

A bible contains two testaments. Each testament contains several books. Each book contains a number of chapters which is consisted of verses. Knowing this fact, the application will show these contents in nested hierarchy style. Shortcuts for navigation must also be provided to provide more convenient way to go to another chapter.

Social networking service

Christians goes to church in reality. They have groups in the church for studying the bible or sharing the daily lives (and sometimes related to the verses). Priest in the church will have presentations and sharing every week. Most of them must have been writing notes about the verses they read, keeping record on what the others said.

It is a good idea to have a virtual church. You may be a member in the church, or just a friend of a churchâ€™s member. You may join the virtual church for keeping in touch to the church and learn more from the church.

So we decided to let the our member to create or join virtual churches. They could also be friend with the other members. Moreover they cold follow other members in order to see their public markers.

In combining the above two, we will have a social-driven knowledge sharing web application, which records the efforts of the priests and christians and shares their efforts to newly joined members!
